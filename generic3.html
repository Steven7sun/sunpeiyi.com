Positionable Bookcase

Inspired by the office lights and the TV remote controller, I decided to make a bookcase that can help people find the position of the book with lights and book number. In this way, the problem of locatinga book is sovled and the weakness of dim light in the bookcase overcome.

For the purpose of remote control for lights, I first used Arduino for programming, and then LEDs, infrared receivers, and remote controllers. The infrared receiver decides which LED light to light up according to the received infrared wave frequency.

Fix the Arduino board to the back of the bookcase, and fix the wire of the strip to the back to prevent disconnection during daily use. And test the light strip again before confirming the wiring to prevent accidents.
